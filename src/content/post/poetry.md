# Words That Live in Me ✦ (´。• ᵕ •。`)

lyrics · poetry · feelings I couldn't say myself

---

Some things are too heavy to carry in your head, too shapeless to put into your own words.
So you find a song. A poem. A line someone wrote at 2am that somehow describes you perfectly.
These are mine. (´ ▽`).。o♡

## ( ˘ ³˘)♥ Take Me In Your Arms

<div style="border:1px solid #333; border-radius:12px; padding:1.5rem; margin:1.5rem 0; line-height:2;">

Take me in your arms  
I fell asleep, ah  
It's been so long since I last slept  

And if you see my tears  
Pretend that you didn't  

Take me in your arms  
And heal my wounds and if i cried  
Nudge me and tell me  
It's shameful to cry  

Take me in your arms  
I want to sleep  
It's been too long since I last slept  

And if you see my tears  
Pretend that you didn't  

I always wanted someone  
Who cries when I cry  
Then I wanted just someone  
To be happy for me  

I used to see people like how I am  
If I got hungry or thirsty  
I never thought that people  
Are never the same  

</div>

> *"I always wanted someone who cries when I cry"* — the moment you stop expecting mirrors and start accepting that people are just… different. hits different every time. (｡•́︿•̀｡)

## ヽ(^o^) Why I Keep These

<div style="display:grid; grid-template-columns:1fr 1fr; gap:1rem; margin:1.5rem 0;">

<div style="border:1px solid #333; border-radius:8px; padding:1rem;">

**They say what I can't**  
Sometimes a lyric understands you better than you understand yourself. I keep these like bookmarks in my own story.

</div>

<div style="border:1px solid #333; border-radius:8px; padding:1rem;">

**They age weirdly well**  
You read them at 14 and feel one thing. At 18 they mean something completely different. Same words, different you.

</div>

<div style="border:1px solid #333; border-radius:8px; padding:1rem;">

**They're honest**  
No performance. No filter. Just someone sitting with a feeling and writing it down. I respect that a lot. (´• ω •`)

</div>

<div style="border:1px solid #333; border-radius:8px; padding:1rem;">

**They remind me I'm not alone**  
Someone wrote these. Someone felt this. That matters more than it probably should. ♡

</div>
