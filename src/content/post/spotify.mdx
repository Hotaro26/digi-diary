I've always been a late night person — not by choice honestly, just how my brain works. (￣▽￣)ノ
It does its best thinking (and its worst overthinking) somewhere between 11pm and 3am.

Music is what makes that hour feel less lonely. These aren't necessarily sad songs —
they're just songs that *understand the hour*. You know what I mean? (´• ω •`)
